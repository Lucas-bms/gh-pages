My Approach

To fully grasp the scope of your project, whether it's a problem to be solved, a system to be developed, an ecosystem to be explored or even an idea, it's imperative to cultivate analytical clarity by delving into the lived experience of all the players who interact with it. In this context of complexity, different approaches enable us to discover systems, interactions, players, uses, etc. and to put hypotheses to the test, thus shedding light on the trajectory to follow to bring your project to fruition and invest in it concretely, from idea to realization.

A methodology resolutely adapted to a global apprehension of the system as well as of users and their environment, in close connection with the product/service, proves to be essential for formulating targeted hypotheses, valid and perennial proposals. This approach encompasses :

  • Field research (systemic analysis of experience, mapping of players, relationships, dynamics, needs and problems);
  • Exploration and definition of sustainable issues (social, environmental, technical);
  • In-depth research into the needs of different users (internal or external to the organization);
  • Collaborative workshops with stakeholders;
  • Development of a roadmap for ongoing strategy, experimentation and optimization of the service and business model.
