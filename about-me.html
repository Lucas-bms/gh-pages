MY VALUES

In my opinion, the forces of design and the designer's posture must support :

  • A design of public utility of the common good and fighting against inequalities ;
  • Design at the service of a sustainable and inclusive society;
  • A participatory design that gives people the means to reflect, to be aware of the impacts and to make decisions based on their experience;
  • A transitional design by creating desirable and sustainable alternative models.
